Our Story

Magnolia Visuals is a Mississippi based creative team serving the southeast, offering full-service production, design, and strategy. We're passionate about bringing stories to life with quality and intentionality.

What We Do

Video Production
From cinematic commercials to heartfelt wedding films, we craft video content that tells your story with clarity, emotion, and impact. Our services include pre-production planning, professional filming, and high-end editing tailored to your brand or occasion.
Photography
We offer styled brand shoots, lifestyle imagery, product photography, athletics, and event coverage. We capture the tone and texture of your story with an editorial eye. View our photo and video gear list.
Design
Our design work ranges from bold visual identities to motion graphics and digital content packages. We help brands express their personality through compelling logos, typography, layouts, and custom assets.
Creative Strategy
With a strong understanding of audience and narrative, we guide your vision from idea to execution. We develop storytelling strategies, campaign content plans, and visual direction to ensure your brand connects and converts.
